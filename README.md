Rockfall 3D is a fast-paced **3D spaceship survival and shooting game** where players dodge falling asteroids while navigating through space. Your mission is simple:
**Avoid incoming rocks, survive as long as possible, and destroy asteroids using your blaster.**

Built using **Unity 6000.2.6f2**, this game serves as a prototype and learning project demonstrating movement, physics, and shooting mechanics.

---

## **Game Overview**

Pilot a spaceship through an asteroid-filled zone where rocks constantly fall from above.
Move freely in 3D space, dodge obstacles, and use your built-in blaster to destroy asteroids before they hit you.

The longer you survive, the higher your score.
